The Joys of Learning HTML

By Disha | Date: May 27, 2025

Introduction

Learning HTML might seem daunting at first, but its truly the foundation of web development. Every webpage you visit, from a simple to a complex social media platform, starts with HTML.

Why Html is Important?

Html(HyperText Markup Language) provides the structure for web content. Without it, there would be no headings, paragraphs, images or liks. It defines the meaning and purpose of your content.

"HTML is the backbone of the internet !!"

Getting started

To begin, all you need is atext editor and web browser. Create a new file, save it with a '.html' extension, and start using your tags. Its that simple.

Conclusion

Embrace HTML, and you'll unlock the door to creating your own corner of the web. Happy Coding!!
